Hangman Game!!

How do you play?

Choose a category. The computer thinks of a word or phrase. The computer will show a number of dashes equivalent to the number of letters in the word. You will try to guess what it is one letter at a time. If you guess a letter that occurs in the word, the computer fills in the appropriate places with the letter. But if the word does not contain the letter you guessed, the computer will take away a life. If your remaining lives reaches zero, the computer wins, but if you guess the word before you run out of lives, you win!!!
